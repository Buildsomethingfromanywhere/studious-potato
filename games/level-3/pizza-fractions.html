Pizza Fractions

How much pizza has toppings?
👨‍🍳

For grown-ups 👋

Practices fractions visually: a pizza is divided into slices and your child names the topped fraction. Starts with halves and quarters, advances to thirds, sixths, and eighths, then mixes in equivalent fractions (2/4 shown as a choice for 1/2). A wrong answer makes the pizza pulse its slices one by one — counting them out.
